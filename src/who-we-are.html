Discover who we are

From refinery to forecourt

As pioneers in the UK's energy transition, EET Fuels is leading the way towards a sustainable future with the nation's first low-carbon refinery. These efforts aim to significantly reduce carbon emissions, aligning with the government's decarbonisation goals and paving the way for a greener tomorrow.  As part of this journey, we stand at a unique crossroads of capability and innovation. We are a leading fuels manufacturer, distributor, and retailer and possess an unparalleled ability to meet the needs of the UK fuel retailers:

1

Quality fuels and security of supply - today and into the future.

We own and operate the Stanlow Refinery and Stanlow Terminals, vital assets to fuels security and a cornerstone of the UK economy. Producing over 4.4 billion litres of diesel, 3 billion litres of petrol annually – around 16% of UK’s transport fuels – Stanlow is among Europe’s most advanced refineries, preparing to lead the transition to low-carbon fuel production.

2

Added value across the
supply chain - from
refinery to forecourt

From Stanlow Refinery to our extensive storage and distribution network – including the UK’s largest independent fuel storage facilities and the strategic UK Oil Pipeline System – and our broad network of service stations, operating under the Essar brand, we are uniquely positioned to deliver unparalleled value throughout every stage of the supply chain.

3

A fresh new approach to fuel and convenience retailing — "Driving Community Convenience"

We are accelerating our transformation towards a better tomorrow through our Essar-branded service stations with a renewed purpose: 'Driving Community Convenience'. This initiative focuses on meeting the needs of local communities, turning our sites into essential hubs for the people we serve. We are committed to delivering on this promise by guaranteeing an experience that is easy for customers, simple for staff and adding exceptional value to retailers.

We invite you to join our growing network and benefit from our secure supply of quality fuels, value driven partnerships, and innovative retail solutions.
