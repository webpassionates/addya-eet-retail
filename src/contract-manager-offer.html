Why partner
with EET Fuels?

As EET Fuels continues to extend its forecourt network across the UK, we are keenly searching for experienced business partners eager to manage our company-operated service stations. Under this business model, you can operate and grow your own business with EET Fuel’s support and take advantage of our competitive pricing and guaranteed income.

Key benefits

Profit management

We optimise income structures and provide cost-effective solutions to enhance profitability, ensuring a transparent model for all stakeholders.

Industry expertise

We provide extensive industry experience and partner with the top convenience retail brands in the UK.

Comprehensive training and support

With a focus on operational excellence and efficiency, we provide the tools and knowledge your team needs, including a detailed operations package that covers both the forecourt and convenience store.

Innovation

Our commitment to technology integration means you'll stay ahead with cutting-edge POS systems, monitoring tools, and engagement platforms, all designed to enhance the forecourt and convenience store experience.

Customer-centric approach

Our dedication to customer satisfaction ensures a positive experience, fostering long-term success for both our partners and customers.

Key responsibilities

Operate the service station in compliance with the legal standards, regulations and terms outlined in the contract with EET Fuels.
Recruit and manage staff to ensure efficient business operations.
Uphold exceptional levels of customer service and adhere to brand standards.
Engage in sales promotions and marketing campaigns established by our brand.
Liaise with the Area Manager designated by EET Fuels to improve the day-to-day operations of the business.
